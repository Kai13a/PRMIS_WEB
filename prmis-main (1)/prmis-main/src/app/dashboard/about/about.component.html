Personnel Record Management System

Information

Personnel Record Management Information System (PRMIS) is a database that maintains and stores confidential and critical information of Batangas State University employees across all campuses. Personnel records contain full information about all employees, such as name, date of birth, marital status, academic qualifications, professional qualifications, previous employment details, and so on. This saves a lot of time during the hiring process and allows for the uploading of details about the user without fear of unauthorized disclosure.
